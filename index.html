Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?
Just click Start Your Order and chat with our AI concierge! It walks you through everything — who the basket is for, their preferences, your budget, and delivery details. No forms to fill out.
How long does it take to receive my basket?
Standard orders typically take 3-7 business days. Rush orders (less than 72 hours) are available based on current volume.
Can I customize everything in the basket?
Your preferences guide the curation! Brandyce personally selects every item. Specific items may vary based on availability and seasonality.
Do you handle food allergies?
Absolutely — we always ask about allergies and dietary restrictions. Items may be sourced from environments with common allergens. An allergen-free guarantee cannot be provided.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Zelle and PayPal (direct invoice). For orders $60 and above, a 50% non-refundable deposit is required before curation begins.
Is personalization included in every tier?
Personalization is included in Enhanced ($60+), Premium ($100), and Signature Luxury ($150+) tiers. Community and Classic tiers do not include it unless you upgrade.
Do you offer delivery?
Yes! We offer both local pickup and delivery. Details are discussed when you place your order.

Chat with our AI concierge to design the perfect personalized gift basket. It takes just a few minutes!
